Chicken Biryani🥘🍗:

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice-based dish made with marinated chicken, basmati rice, and a blend of spices. The dish is layered with rice, chicken, and spices, and cooked to perfection, resulting in a fragrant and mouth-watering meal.







Parotta🔥:

One of India's most beloved and creamy vegetarian dishes! Palak Paneer is a popular North Indian dish made with: Palak: Spinach puree Paneer: Indian cheese, typically made from cow's or buffalo's milk The dish is creamy, rich, and flavorful, with the spinach puree infused with spices, garlic, and ginger, and the paneer adding a nice texture and flavor.







Fried Rice🔥:

A classic Chinese dish that's easy to make, customizable, and delicious! Fried Rice is a Chinese dish made from cooked rice, vegetables, and often meat or seafood, stir-fried together in a wok or large skillet.Fried Rice is a Chinese dish that originated in the Qing Dynasty (1644-1912). It was created as a way to use leftover rice and other ingredients.
